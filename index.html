Privacy Policy for Guldasta e Najat

Last updated: June 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Guldasta e Najat ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from users:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data (only while the app is running in the foreground)
  • Device and usage data (e.g., crash logs, device model, OS version)

1.1 Prominent Disclosure for Location Access

Guldasta e Najat requires access to your device's location to provide accurate Islamic prayer (Azan) times based on your geographical position. Location data is collected only when the app is actively used and is not stored or shared with any third-party services without your consent.

You will be prompted to allow or deny this permission at runtime and can manage this permission anytime from your device settings.

A prominent in-app disclosure is shown before any personal or sensitive user data is collected. This disclosure explains what data is collected, how it is used, and includes options for the user to accept or decline. Data collection begins only after the user explicitly provides consent.

1.2 Prominent Disclosure Example

Before collecting any location data, Guldasta e Najat will display a clear prompt inside the app that states:

"Guldasta e Najat requests your location permission to provide accurate Azan and prayer times based on your current position. Your location data will only be used while the app is in use and will not be shared without your consent."

You will have the choice to Allow or Deny this permission. Data collection starts only after you give explicit consent.

You can also manage or revoke location permissions anytime from your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve and maintain app functionality
  • Communicate with users (if needed)
  • Monitor app performance and troubleshoot issues
